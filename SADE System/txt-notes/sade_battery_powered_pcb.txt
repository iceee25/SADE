SADE Door Lock System - Battery Powered PCB Design
=================================================

BATTERY PACK SPECIFICATIONS:
- Configuration: 8x 18650 Li-ion cells (2S4P or 4S2P)
- Voltage Options:
  * 2S4P: 7.4V nominal (6.0V-8.4V range), 10,400mAh capacity
  * 4S2P: 14.8V nominal (12.0V-16.8V range), 5,200mAh capacity
- Recommended: 4S2P for better voltage regulation
